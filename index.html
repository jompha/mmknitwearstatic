High Quality Clothing

All clothes are cut stitched and finished by hand and undergo a five stage quality control procedure to ensure satisfaction. Throughout manufacture all stages are quality checked to ensure the highest standards are met.

01

Fabric Sourcing

Fabrics are sourced to match your requirements for composition and feel great texture. We can also dye fabrics to meet your exact colour specifications and give materials additional treatments for softness.

02

Prototypes & Sampling

For each order we create a prototype for your approval, allowing you to test and refine the functionality of your design. Once your samples have been approved, we will commence bulk manufacture.

03

Bulk Manufacture

Bulk manufacture takes place on our production line, where all items are made for order and quality checked throughout. Quality checks also take place in the finishing stages, before your items are packaged for delivery.

04

Our Journey

Established in 2001, MM Knitwear is one of the top knitwear manufacturer based in Bangladesh. The company has specialised in manufacturing organic cotton and fast fashion knitwear for global brands, independent boutiques and specialist retailers. MM Knitwear is one of the Bangladesh’s leading clothing manufacturers, producing garments of the highest quality. We offer a full scale manufacturing solution including a range of services to meet our buyers specification.
