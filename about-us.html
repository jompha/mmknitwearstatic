About Us

At M.M. Knitwear Ltd. we aim to continue developing with the latest technologies available in manufacturing to accommodate large orders to satisfy our clients at best. Our strength as a private company enables us to create an organic environment that provides opportunity for our people to grow as we do. M.M. Knitwear Ltd. has already been recognised by many influential figureheads in Bangladesh and are well on our way to becoming an industrial institution in one of the world’s rising countries.

  • Year of establishment : July 2001.
  • Estamited turnover for year 2020 : 65.00 Million U.S Dollars.
  • Production capacity : 1,45,000 Pcs/Day for basic T-Shirt/Tops and 1,20,000 Pcs/Day for average styles.

Quality Policy

We strive to achieve customer excellence by exceeding expectation of product quality, product performance, and delivery. We are committed to implement best quality systems, which conform to international standards. Our crusade to build a sustainable business with continuous improvement, quality management, attention to detail and strict commitment to buyer’s requirements is gathering its pace. Thank you to all our clients for making this dream a reality.

Welfare Facilities

  • Induction of workers welfare committee (aimed at improving workers morale, job satisfaction and attendance)
  • Canteen service
  • Subsidized lunch for employees
  • Free snacks on duty
  • Full time free medical service provided by two professional doctor in house
  • Child care center for employees, annual picnic.
  • In house prayer facilities for male & female.
  • Annual cultural celebration

Compliance

Our factory maintains a full compliance of international laws, Human rights, Social compliance, Technical Compliance, C-Tpat, Labor Law and other rules and instruction by concern authority. 

Employee & Social Commitment

As an organisation our strength is focused on our employees and development of their skills, enabling it to offer the best products and the best services to its customers. Through sponsorship we also support local, regional or national sports and charity actions. 

Sustainable Development

Since the beginning we have always strived to provide the best quality products to our customers that are sourced and manufactured ethically. We are continuously looking to work more sustainably to reduce plastic consumption. All our facilities strictly maintains OEKO-TEX and other compliances to reduce the use of energy consumption, waste management and safe working environment.
